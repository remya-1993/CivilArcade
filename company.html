About Us

Welcome to Civil Arcade, experience in construction consultancy and development services. We specialize in delivering comprehensive solutions for all your construction needs, including building
plan preparation, 3D visualizations, online submissions via K-SMART, land type change processes (Tharam Mattam), and survey & sketch services. With a commitment to precision, innovation,
and client satisfaction, we bring expertise and professionalism to every project. Whether you're planning your dream home, managing regulatory approvals, or transforming land use, our skilled
team ensures seamless execution at every stage. At Civil Arcade, we aim to simplify complex processes, helping clients navigate the technical and legal aspects of construction with confidence.

Services

 Building Plan Preparation:
We design functional and aesthetically appealing building plans tailored to your specific requirements while adhering to local building codes and regulations.
 3D Drawings & Visualizations::
Our advanced 3D modeling services bring your vision to life by creating realistic representations of your project, helping you visualize the final outcome
before construction begins.
 Online Submissions (via K-SMART):
Navigating the digital realm of construction approvals can be challenging. Our expertise in the K-SMART platform ensures a smooth and
hassle-free submission process for obtaining building permits and other approvals.
 Land Type Change (Tharam Mattam):
We guide you through the intricate procedures of converting land types, ensuring compliance with local laws and regulations to facilitate
the optimal use of your property.
 Survey & Sketch Services:
Accurate surveying and detailed sketch preparation are the backbone of any successful construction project. Our skilled surveyors and technical team
provide precise measurements and documentation to set the foundation for your project.
